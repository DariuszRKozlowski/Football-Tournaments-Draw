In FIFA World Cup take part 32 countries from all federations.
Before the draw teams are divided into 4 pots (8 national teams per each pot).
To the first pot goes:
- National team of tournament host country,
- National teams from countries with the best position in FIFA Ranking.*
Teams into other pots are allocated only by FIFA Ranking.

From pot 1 to pot 4, each team from single pot goes to one group from A to H.**
In the same group can not play together national teams from the same federation.***

By political issues, in the same group can not play together national teams of:
a) Russia and Ukraine,
b) Russia and Kosovo, 
c) Spain and Gibraltar, 
d) Armenia and Azerbaijan,
e) Kosovo and Bosnia & Herzegovina,
f) Kosovo and Serbia.

* If host country national team has also one of the best position in FIFA Ranking,
its place in pot is taken by national team with the next best ranking.
** Host country is automatically allocated to group A.
*** Exceptions are national teams from UEFA (European Federation). In the same
group can play maximum 2 teams sfrom this federation.
